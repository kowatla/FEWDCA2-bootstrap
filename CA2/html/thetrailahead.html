Big Tech

Most of these selections are found in Silicon Valley with the exception of Duolingo

Netflix is a really good entry point into FAANG companies as it is relatively small but still provides really good opportunities for innovation and creativity. Netflix is written in Python and makes for easy cross platform development.

Apple & Google are arguably the most successful tech companies and are really desirable environments to work in. Both companies have incredible resources and employees that work in them. They have a huge emphasis on human resource and development and offer many attractive perks to work with. All in all working in these giants would be a privilege that I would not resist. The main difference between these giants are the organization structures. Apple has operates in a centralized and unified structure while google operates with a decentralized and diversified structure. Both systems have their advantages and I hope to experience working in both companies in the future.

A pioneer in the tech industry, Microsoft has stood the test of time and is continuing to innovate in new technologies like augmented reality and improving their existing software. It also offers a good work-life balance through offers like a robust benefits package and high salary.
Small Startups

Most of these selections are found in The Big Apple with the exception of Duolingo

A small startup in NYC that is also a Public Benefit Corporation, Kickstarter places more emphasize on the product and bringing good to the public rather than raw profits. This people over profit priority is something that is prominent in Big Tech but for a small startup to organize itself for this commitment is something that I am quite fascinated about.

This progressive workspace offers the best employee benefits ranging from Unlimited vacation to Paid sabbaticals for tenured employees, skillshare offers a workspace where employees can feel comfortable working for and in.

Truly one of the most progressive startups in the world, Squarespace has revolutionized the work environment with award winning workplace environments. I believe that at Squarespace I would be able to hone my skills and learn whist keeping a healthy social life

Founded on the principles of a workplace that makes its employees feel happy and content, Duolingo houses one of the most progressive workplaces with wide diversity and the drive to develop a service that changes the world for a better place. Duolingo is one of the most desired places to work at
